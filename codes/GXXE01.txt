These codes will allow you to customize the stats of any Pokémon
in your arsenal. To use them, convert the number you want for each
stat into Hex, then place them in the code and encrypt (Example:
999 in Hex is 03E7). The first line will modify the Pokémon's HP,
the second line will modify Attack, third line will modify Defence,
fourth line Special Attack, fifth line Special Defence, and sixth
line Speed. If you do not wish to modify every stat, or only want
to modify one, simply remove the lines you don't want to use before
encrypting. NOTE: Do not go over 32767 (7FFF in Hex), or your stat
will go to an insanely large number that you did not want.
