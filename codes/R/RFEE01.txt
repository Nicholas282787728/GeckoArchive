I should make a note, these are the results I got for playing the game on normal mode with widescreen on. Certain units like Ulki and Janaff might appear in a later spot in data depending on how many units you defeated on the map. I'll point out though that every unit up to the last Aura are in 1 slot after another from each other. The xp code tells you what slot in the data they're at..example: 048A460C "81"142763 That is Tibarn's slot in the data, while the last Aura is down at 50. Look at the values for each unit starting from Nolan's codes and down and you'll notice they are 3EC(hex) values away from each other. I hope this helps you understand a little about how Fire Emblem does some of it's stuff.

Put these at the end of any of the "player-controlled" codes to change what side they're on.
48 = other
54 = partner
3C = enemy
30 = player
Also, the skill modifier codes are in slots, the first line is slot 1 down to slot 6. There are even more slots, only 8 values ahead of each other.
